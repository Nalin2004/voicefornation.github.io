INFORMATION

India launched a new social media campaign to amplify PM Modi's message of

'Vocal For Local' and self-reliability as the country emerges from

the shadows of Covid-19.The campaign 'Be Indian Buy Indian' will

encourage all Indians to buy 'Made in India' products by using only some

specially curated creatives on the social media platforms.

Saurabh Agarwal, director, Kamdhenu, said, “The fight against the

pandemic COVID-19 is a joint effort by the government and citizens of

the country. Kamdhenu Paints, as a responsible Indian Company has been

doing its bit in contributing to the fight.

Vocal for Local’ is a concept which dates back to the era of the

Swadeshi movement which stared in 1905 as part of the Indian independence

movement.

So ‘Vocal for Local’ would have been a brilliant strategy for some

sectors in India but definitely not in most of the sectors. While ‘Vocal

for Local’ is a great idea, we have to recognise that the realities of the

global environment have changed significantly. It is no longer possible to

create globally competitive products without access to globally competitive

resources, and without creating globally competitive synergies.

India is like the rest of the world who is certainly going through a

tough phase but will still shine due to its vast pool of educated talent.
